ACCURAY
01

Project Challenge

Objective

Accuray International wanted to create a customer experience for their exhibition booth at ESTRO 2021.

The experience was to be present the Accuray brand across multiple screens and drew booth visitors into an immersive experience.

02

Design Approach

Objective

GBP recommended using a multiple screen arrangement as a single canvas.

A custom 3D animation would traverse the canvas ensuing full utilization of screen real estate.

The animaton would showcase the Accuray logo experience through multiple textures

  • Wood
  • Liquid
  • Diamond
  • Metal Voxels
  • LED Lights

The animation projected key brand messaging for customers.

GBP created a storyboard for the animation that clearly outlined the steps for the entire event flow.

03

Solution - Outcome

Objective

The multi screen animation was the mainstay at Accurays Estro booth

The animation played across seamlessly combining multi-screens

We were humbled by the appreciation received -

  • “Thank you for the great and very creative support! Very glad to work with you all!” - Sancie Nakarat, Senior Director, Global Marketing and Communications
  • “Your work for Accuray has been a game changer for us!” - Philippe Degreze, VP Marketing, EIMEA
